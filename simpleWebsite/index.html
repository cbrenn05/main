Background

Castiel is an angel who is currently inhabiting the vessel of Jimmy Novak. He first appeared in Supernatural season 4, after rescuing Dean Winchester from hell. Castiel has helped the Winchesters in their adventures ever since, costing him a lot along the way. He became a god, then a human, died and was resurrected, and was betrayed by his own species. His reputation is known to all angels as either a hero or a villain. Castiel remains an important figure within Team Free Will (Dean Winchester, Samuel Winchester, Castiel Novak) and he is committed to protecting humanity, no matter what dangers he encounters.
